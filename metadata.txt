about=Create shareable Google Maps and Street View links directly from QGIS.
 It converts any clicked map point to WGS84 and builds the URL for you.
 Key features:
 - One click to copy the link or open it in your browser
 - Choose Google Maps or Street View as the default action
 - Automatic reprojection to WGS84 from any layer CRS
 - Configurable zoom level for Maps and Street View
 - Toolbar button remembers your last choice
 - Address search: from the dropdown menu, search by address (Nominatim) to center the map on a place
 Perfect for quickly sharing precise locations or opening field navigation.
 Translations: English (default) and Italian (it).
